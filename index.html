About Company
We grow, therefore we are

Mohandesin-e Barman Borna-ye Rasht (MBBR) Company was founded in 2018. From the beginning, we kept an eye on the latest trends in technology. At first, we focused on designing and manufacturing industrial machinery for different companies. But now we have three departments in our small but motivated company.

If you are looking for something new, you are in the right place. We use the abilities of our three following departments to make connections between different fields of science and industry.

  • Mechanical end Electrical Engineering
  • Software Development
  • Training

We believe our young engineers can persuade you to become our regular customer.

MBBR has focused on developing the latest technology, and you can join us regardless of where you are located.
